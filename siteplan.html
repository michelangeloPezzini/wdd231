Website Plan

Michelangelo Photography

Site Name

Michelangelo Photography

This name was selected because it represents my personal identity as a photographer and showcases my photography hobby. Using my name helps create a personal brand and allows visitors to easily recognize my work.

Site Purpose

The purpose of this website is to showcase my photography hobby and share my personal work. Visitors will be able to learn about me, explore my photography portfolio, and contact me. The site will include three main pages: Home, Portfolio, and Contact.

Site Structure

  • Home: Introduction about me and my photography passion.
  • Portfolio: Gallery displaying my photography work.
  • Contact: Page where visitors can contact me.
